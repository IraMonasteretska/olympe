Composable Edge
Computing Solutions

Unlock the Power of Real-Time
Data at the Edge

Olympe, in partnership with LineBreak, enables enterprises to harness the value of edge data through customizable solutions. We empower real-time decision making by processing data where it is generated. Our integrated edge-to-cloud architecture reduces cloud costs, ensures compliance, and leverages AI locally.

Reduce Cloud Costs

Leverage Olympe's distributed processing capabilities to analyze data and run workflows at the edge. This localized processing minimizes the data that must be pushed to the cloud, reducing cloud egress fees by up to 80%.

Drive Local Actions

Get microsecond latency insights by analyzing edge data in real-time at the source. Olympe enables instant response based on live edge data patterns, driving actions like shutting down unsafe equipment.

Enhance Security

Adhere to data security and compliance policies by keeping sensitive data within the edge network. Local edge processing with Olympe ensures data privacy without the risks of moving regulated data to the cloud.

Future-Proof Investments

Easily integrate new edge data sources and hardware into your environment over time. Olympe’s open architecture ensures you can onboard emerging technologies and scale smoothly as edge computing evolves.

Our Integrated Capabilities Deliver Composable Edge Success

We have partnered with LineBreak, a global edge solution group, to deliver purpose-built edge solutions tailored to meet your strategic data processing and analytics needs.

Together, Olympe and LineBreak provide enterprise-grade capabilities to optimize your edge infrastructure:

  • Consulting Services: Linebreak experts assess your edge opportunities and build data-driven business cases to inform your edge roadmap and guide strategic decision-making.
  • Proof of Value: We rapidly validate edge concepts by prototyping solutions on Olympe. LineBreak provides dedicated hands-on expertise for your proof of value.
  • Edge Platform: LineBreak delivers cloud-based orchestration, management, and security purpose-built for enterprise edge infrastructure - providing a robust foundation.
  • Olympe Platform: We build and deploy the solutions that extract value from your edge, with seamless integration from the edge to cloud to core.
