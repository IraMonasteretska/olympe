Unified Procurement Dashboard
at a Leading FMCG Company

A top global FMCG company had over 400 buyers across heterogeneous procurement systems, making it difficult to get a unified view of purchases and suppliers. By consolidating 30+ data sources into an Olympe-powered dashboard, they were able to revolutionize procurement operations. The tailored solution delivered 10x ROI by reducing workload by 50% and cutting vendor costs.

The Problem

The company had a complex procurement technology landscape with 400+ buyers using dozens of disjointed systems. With no centralized portal, they lacked visibility into purchases, suppliers, and spending trends. Extracting actionable insights was extremely difficult across siloed applications. This resulted in highly manual, inefficient processes that wasted resources and time. They desperately needed a solution to unify their data and enhance decision-making.

The Solution

Leveraging Olympe's composable approach, the company built an actionable dashboard that consolidated 30+ different data sources into a single view. Real-time analytics provided visibility into spending patterns, vendor performance, and other key procurement metrics empowering data-driven decisions. By revolutionizing how buyers accessed and leveraged information, Olympe enabled them to optimize costs and supplier relationships. The tailored solution was delivered in just 9 months.

The Results

The impact was immediate and immense. By reducing manual workload by 50% and cutting vendor costs, the company achieved 10x ROI rapidly. Frontend systems were consolidated from 10+ down to just 1 unified interface, creating simplicity for 500+ users. Adoption was seamless due to the solution perfectly aligning with their processes and pain points.

Streamlined Sourcing Requests at
a Leading CPG Company

A major CPG company automated and simplified sourcing requests, saving hundreds of hours in procurement throughput time.

The Challenge

The procurement department received hundreds of sourcing requests per month via fragmented channels like email and phone calls. Requesters struggled to leverage their Ariba and Fairmarkit buying platforms, resulting in manual work for buyers, lengthy processes, and incomplete data. This caused production planning delays, compliance issues, and poor traceability.

The Solution

Leveraging Olympe, the company built an easy-to-use sourcing request portal. It aggregates all needed information per business rules to auto-populate and submit orders to buying platforms. This unified and automated solution replaced previously disjointed systems.

The Results

Streamlining requests into a single entry point saved hundreds of training hours with thousands of requesters. Buyers reduced manual work 20% through automation. Compliance improved with enforced business policies. Enhanced traceability drove significant annual tax savings.
